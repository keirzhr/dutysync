Data Privacy & Your Information

Understanding how your contact information is used in DutySync

How Your Information May Be Shared

When organizations or individuals use DutySync to manage their workforce and payroll, they may upload or input employee contact information such as names, email addresses, and phone numbers as part of their duty logging and payroll management process.

If you're an employee whose information has been added to the system by your employer or manager, this is to facilitate:

Legitimate Business Purposes:

  • Work hour tracking and attendance management
  • Payslip generation and salary computation
  • Employee profile management within the organization
  • Communication regarding work schedules and compensation
  • Record-keeping for compliance with labor regulations

How We Protect Your Data

DutySync is committed to protecting your personal information. Here's how we ensure your data security:

Security Measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure Firebase authentication and database structure
  • Access controls - users only see their own data
  • No sharing of personal data with third parties for marketing
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights & Control

You have full rights regarding your personal information in DutySync:

What You Can Do:

  • View and update your personal information anytime
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your data for personal records
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

Your Data Belongs to You

DutySync does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with external parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used solely for the payroll and time management services you or your organization has signed up for. You maintain ownership and control of your information at all times.
